Welcome to Ashesi Univeristy

The Ashesi Experiential Transcript captures studentsâ€™ endeavours in ways that demonstrate their assimilation of the teaching of Ashesi University's learning goals: ethical, entrepreneurial leaders in Africa with critical thinking skills, effective communication skills, practical experience, concern for others and the courage to transform the continent.

Under Ashesi's core pillars of Scholarship, Leadership and Citizenship, students will provide information on their transformation through self-initiated experiences during their four-year university education on an annual basis. This information will be verified and recorded in an official transcript from the university. The Academic Affairs Department reserves the right to determine what information appears on the final transcript. Each of the three pillars is defined and has elements and metrics to guide the collection of this information. The metrics serve as examples of the type of information anticipated and students may provide other information they deem relevant to the pillars.

All information provided should be supported with evidence in the form of pictures, videos, reports and the like as applicable. For an activity to count, students must have alerted a faculty or staff member of their intent and had some direct or indirect oversight before embarking on it. Such faculty or staff should serve as at least one of the references required.
